Frequently asked questions

  • How to make an order using the basket?

    1. Select the desired quantity of items for each product.
    2. Click on "Add to the basket".
    3. Go to the cart using the icon in the navigation bar.
    4. Confirm the cart.
    5. Provide the requested information.
    6. Confirm the order.

  • How to make a direct order?

    1. Select the desired quantity of items for each product.
    2. Click on "Order directly".
    3. Provide the requested information.
    4. Confirm the order.
