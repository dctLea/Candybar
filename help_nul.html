To place an order using the basket, start by selecting the desired quantity of items for each product. Once selected, click on "Add to the basket" to add the chosen items. Navigate to the cart using the icon in the navigation bar, and then proceed to confirm the cart. Following that, provide the required information and finalize the order.
For a direct order, the process involves selecting the desired quantity of items for each product and then clicking on "Order directly." Subsequently, provide the necessary information and confirm the order. This streamlined process offers a quicker way to make a purchase without the intermediate step of adding items to the basket.
